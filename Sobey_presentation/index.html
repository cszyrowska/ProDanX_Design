A Blueprint For Sobey Fitness

Creating a digital home for the FitFam.

An architectural walkthrough of a custom platform designed to centralise your bookings, cut out group-chat noise, and strengthen your community.

1. The Home Hub

A private social network.

Rather than opening up to a boring calendar, the app opens to a living, breathing community feed dedicated entirely to Sobey Fitness.

  • Coach Visibility: Dan and Lisa's urgent announcements are "pinned" to the top, guaranteeing they are seen.
  • Organic Meetups: Members can easily share a post to organize a weekend hike or a morning sunrise walk.
  • Safe Space: A distraction-free environment away from the noise of Facebook and WhatsApp.
2. Class Bookings

Everything in one place.

No more sending members to external websites, web-browsers, or clunky third-party apps just to book an Urban Gym session.

  • The Ecosystem: Members chat, view updates, and book classes all under one single roof.
  • Clean Logistics: A fast, chronological schedule that removes the friction of securing a spot.
  • Optional Social Draw: We can show avatars of friends who have booked the same class, creating a natural pull to fill empty slots.
3. Dedicated Chats

Cut the noise.
Organise effortlessly.

Information gets buried when you are bombarded with texts 24/7 in massive WhatsApp groups. This solves that problem entirely.

  • Event Interest: A member posts a hangout idea to the main feed (e.g., "Saturday Beach Swim").
  • Auto-Chat Creation: When others click "Join", they are instantly placed into a separate, dedicated chat just for that event.
  • Focused Details: Questions about parking or times stay inside that one chat, keeping the main feed clean.

Smart Tech

Working smarter, not harder.

We can weave subtle AI into the background of the app to protect your personal time while keeping members highly engaged.

🤖

The "Dan" Bot

An automated assistant trained to answer basic FAQs (class times, pricing). It replies instantly in your exact tone of voice, saving you hours of texting.

🤝

Smart Matchmaking

If a new member is nervous about attending Brute Camp alone, the AI can match them with a veteran member for a pre-class chat to break the ice.

🛡️

Human Escalation

The AI handles the repetitive noise, but always leaves a button for the member to request a direct message with you if they need real human coaching.

Looking Ahead

Optional Expansions

The goal is to start simple. However, when you are ready, the platform is built to seamlessly support these future additions.

✅

Personalised Tasks

Set members daily homework outside of class. Whether it's a 10-minute walk, a hydration goal, or a mobility routine, it keeps them engaged on rest days.

📔

Progress Journals

Instead of just logging reps, members can track how they *felt* after a session. A qualitative mental health check-in that aligns with your wellness approach.

🏆

Challenge Streaks

Automated trackers that celebrate consistency. When a member hits a 10-class streak, the app prompts them to share their win directly to the Home Hub.
