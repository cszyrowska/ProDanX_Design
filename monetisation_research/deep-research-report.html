The revenue models that fit your app best

Setup and launch fee

This is the most natural first revenue stream for your business. Even if your codebase is modular and reusable, each client still needs branding, configuration, onboarding, testing, and app-store preparation. This is a strong profit model because your real delivery cost should fall over time as your internal app library grows. In other words, your sale price stays fairly healthy while your build time drops.

This is also the most scalable model because once a client is live, most of the work becomes maintenance rather than rebuild work. In a modular system, that usually means very strong margins over time.

Which ideas are strongest and which should come later

The strongest immediate model is a three-part core:

  1. one-time launch fee,
  2. monthly platform fee,
  3. small payment margin on bookings and memberships.

  • upfront cash,
  • predictable recurring income,
